##  Project Overview
[This project focuses on developing a strategic digital marketing plan for a kitchen showroom that specializes in designing and selling modern, customized kitchens. Many customers struggle to find kitchen solutions that combine quality, functionality, stylish design, and affordable pricing all in one place. Additionally, the showroom faces challenges in standing out in a highly competitive market and effectively reaching its target audience.

This project aims to solve these challenges by applying structured digital marketing strategy that increases brand awareness, generates qualified leads, and drives showroom visits and sales. The strategy will be presented in a professional presentation formatto provide a comprehensive and actionable roadmap for business growth.]
