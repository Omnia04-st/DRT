##  Project Overview
This project focuses on developing a strategic digital marketing plan for a kitchen showroom that specializes in designing and selling modern, customized kitchens. Many customers struggle to find kitchen solutions that combine quality, functionality, stylish design, and affordable pricing all in one place. Additionally, the showroom faces challenges in standing out in a highly competitive market and effectively reaching its target audience.

This project aims to solve these challenges by applying structured digital marketing strategy that increases brand awareness, generates qualified leads, and drives showroom visits and sales. The strategy will be presented in a professional presentation formatto provide a comprehensive and actionable roadmap for business growth.
---

## Project Objectives
- Strengthen brand positioning by increasing engagement rate by 30% while maintaining a consistent premium visual identity within 3 months.
  
- Expand a targeted audience by growing followers by 25% within 4 months, focusing on high-intent furniture buyers.
  
- Generate 50+ qualified monthly inquiries through WhatsApp and Direct Messages within 3 months.
  
- Enhance product perception by publishing 12–16 lifestyle-focused posts monthly to increase content interaction by 20% within 3 months.

  -Increase social-media-driven sales by 15% within 6 months through strategic campaigns and social proof.
---
##  Project Scope
- This project focuses on developing a comprehensive digital marketing strategy for the kitchen showroom using the SOSTAC framework. The scope includes analyzing the current market position, identifying target audiences, defining measurable marketing objectives, and creating strategic recommendations for social media presence, paid advertising, and lead generation.]  
- The project also covers content planning, customer journey mapping, and performance measurement through defined KPIs.]  
-However, the scope does not include the physical production process of kitchens, financial restructuring, operational management, or direct sales execution. The focus is strictly on the strategic marketing planning and presentation of a structured growth roadmap.]  
 ---
   >  **Final Deliverable:** The final deliverable is a professional presentation outlining a complete SOSTAC marketing strategy for the kitchen showroom, providing a clear, actionable roadmap to increase brand awareness, generate leads, and drive sales.]

## Project Plan (5 Weeks)

###  Week 1 — Market Research & Analysis]
-Analyze the market, competitors, and target audience to understand the current position of the kitchen showroom.
### Week 2 — Objectives & Strategic Direction
- Define clear SMART objectives and determine the brand positioning and overall marketing strategy
### Week 3 —Tactical Planning
- Develop detailed marketing tactics including content strategy, advertising approach, and customer journey planning.
### Week 4 —Action & Implementation Plan
-Create a structured execution plan with timeline, responsibilities, and key performance indicators (KPIs).
### Week 5 — Final Strategy Presentation
- Compile and present a professional SOSTAC-based marketing strategy ready for implementation.
